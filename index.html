Jetflix

The brief for this third project was to build a full-stack app using Express and Mongo for the back end and React JS for the front end. Our idea was to recreate Netflix with holiday destinations instead of movies and an interactive side where users could add pictures of their own holidays similar to Instagram.

Rijksmuseum

This was a 48 hour ‘React-athon’ building a full-stack app using a public API. We worked on this project together using live share and built a virtual Rijksmuseum allowing users to explore the collections that are displayed in the museum. The app has different features such as viewing the collections by room, ‘build your own collection’, toggle the language between Dutch and English and a text to speech audio play option.
